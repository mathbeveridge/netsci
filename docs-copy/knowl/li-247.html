Item 12.3.b.

Let's give a recipe for creating a graph on the vertices \([n]\text{.}\) For each pair of vertices, we must decide whether or not the corresponding edge is in our graph. So we must make \({n \choose 2}\) yes/no decisions, and these decisions are made independently of one another. Therefore, the total number of graphs is

\begin{equation*} 2^{n \choose 2}. \end{equation*}
