Let \(D=(V,E)\) be a weakly connected digraph with strongly connected components \(S_1, S_2, \ldots, S_k\text{.}\) Create a new digraph \(G\) with vertices \(V(G) = \{s_1, s_2, \ldots, s_k \}\) where arc \((s_i,s_j) \in E(G)\) if and only if there is at least one arc from \(S_i\) to \(S_j\) in the original digraph \(D\text{.}\)
