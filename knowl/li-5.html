Item 1.1.4.a.

A "simple digraph" is a digraph without loops and without multiple arcs. You are allowed to have both \(uv\) and \(vu\) in a digraph. There are

\begin{equation*} n (n-1) = (n)_2 \end{equation*}

ways to create an arc. We have \(n\) choices for the starting vertex and \(n-1\) choices for the target vertex.
