Chapter 1 Graphs and Digraphs

Let \(G=(V,E)\) be an undirected graph. Prove the "handshaking lemma," which states that

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle{2|E| = \sum_{v \in V} \deg(v)}\text{.} \end{equation*}

7. Tournaments.

A tournament \(T\) is transitive if whenever \((u,v)\) and \((v,w)\) are arcs in \(T\text{,}\) then \((u,w)\) is also an arc of \(T\text{.}\)

  1. Draw all the non-isomorphic transitive tournaments on 3 vertices and on 4 vertices.

  2. Prove that a tournament \(T\) is transitive if and only if \(T\) has no (directed) cycles. (Hint: prove \(\Longrightarrow\) by contradiction: pick a cycle of shortest length, then show there is an even shorter cycle!)
