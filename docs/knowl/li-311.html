Item 19.1.a.

For each of our basic centrality measures (degree centrality, closeness centrality, betweenness centrality), prove that if you add the edge \((u,v)\) to \(G\) then the centrality of vertex \(u\) does not decrease. (Mathematically, we say that the centrality of \(u\) is weakly increasing for edge additions at \(u\text{.}\))
