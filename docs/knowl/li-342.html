Item 19.6.e.

Finally, find the value \(r^*\) so that \(w(r^*)=1/2\) and its corresponding \(x_{r^*}\text{.}\) The interpretation: the wealthiest \(r^*\) fraction of the population owns half of the total wealth. In order to be among these super-elite people, your net worth must be at least \(x_{r^*}\text{.}\)
