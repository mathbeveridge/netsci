Item 18.9.b.

Let \(A\) be a symmetric matrix, so that \(A = A^{\top}\text{.}\) Let \(\lambda\) and \(\mu\) be distinct nonzero eigenvalues with corresponding (right) eigenvectors \(\bu\) and \(\bv\text{,}\) so that \(A \bu = \lambda \bu\) and \(A \bv = \lambda \bv\text{.}\) Prove that \((A \bu) \cdot \bv =0\text{.}\)
