Solution 1.

  1. For each of the matrix equations above, as \(t \rightarrow \infty\text{,}\) we converge to a vector in the direction of the largest eigenvector. In other words, the hub score \(\bh\) satisfies

    \begin{equation*} A \, A^{\top} \, \bh= \lambda \bh \end{equation*}
    where \(\lambda\) is the largest eigenvalue of \(A \, A^{\top}\text{.}\) Meanwhile, the authority score \(\ba\) satisfies
    \begin{equation*} A^{\top} \, A \, \ba= \mu \ba \end{equation*}
    where \(\mu\) is the largest eigenvalue of \(A^{\top} \,A\text{.}\)

  2. We have \(\lambda=\mu\) because

    \begin{equation*} \lambda \bh = AA^{\top} h = AA^{\top} (A \ba) = A (A^{\top} A \ba) = \mu A \ba = \mu \bh. \end{equation*}
