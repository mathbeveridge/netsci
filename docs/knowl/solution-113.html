Solution 1.

Let \(N = {n \choose 2}\text{.}\) We have

\begin{align*} \langle m \rangle &= \sum_{m = 0}^{N} m {N \choose m} p^m (1-p)^{N - m}\\ &= \sum_{m = 1}^{N} m \frac{N!}{m! (N-m)!} p^m (1-p)^{N - m}\\ &= p N \sum_{m = 1}^{N} m \frac{(N-1)!}{(m-1)! (N-m)!} p^(m-1) (1-p)^{N - m}\\ &= pN (p + (1-p))^{N-1} \, = p N = p {n \choose 2}. \end{align*}
