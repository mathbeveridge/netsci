Solution 1.
\begin{equation*} \E[X] = \frac{22}{7} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \E[X^2] = 2^2 \cdot \frac{1}{7} + 3^2 \cdot \frac{4}{7} + 4^2 \cdot \frac{2}{7} = \frac{4+36+32}{7} = \frac{72}{7} \end{equation*}

and therefore

\begin{equation*} \var(X) = \E[X^2] - \E[X]^2 = \frac{72}{7} - \left( \frac{22}{7} \right)^2 = \frac{20}{49}. \end{equation*}
